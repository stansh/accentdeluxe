Accent Deluxe

Welcome to our website!

Accent Deluxe is a jazz ensemble based in the Twin Cities, MN, blending sounds from across the world into a vibrant and soulful experience. Drawing inspiration from traditional jazz, gypsy jazz, Italian melodies, bossa nova, and Latin rhythms, the group creates a unique and dynamic sound that feels both classic and fresh.
